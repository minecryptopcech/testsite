Privacy Policy

Policy Statement

Match Systems Solutions Pte Ltd (hereafter referred to as “Match Systems” or “the Company”) is dedicated to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of information entrusted to it. As part of this fundamental obligation, Match Systems is committed to the appropriate protection and use of personal information, also referred to as “confidential/personal data,” that has been collected from its stakeholders.
The objective of this Privacy Policy is to inform you about our role and responsibilities as controllers of your personal data. Please review this Privacy Policy carefully and ensure that you understand it. Your acceptance of our Privacy Policy is deemed to occur upon your first use of our website and services. This policy may be updated from time-to-time and applies to the activities described below.
If at any time you wish to send us any queries regarding this policy and how we manage your personal information, please contact us at: info@matchsystems.com.

“We/Us/Our” – means Match Systems Solutions Pte Ltd, a private limited company, registered in Singapore having its legal address at 10 Anson Road #20-05, International Plaza, 079903 Singapore.
“You/Your/Customer” – means any natural or legal person who may access our site or create a working relationship with us via our website.

Safe Retention of Your Personal Data

To ensure the safety of your personal data, we have implemented the following organizational and IT measures:
  • Critical or sensitive network equipment is housed in secure areas with appropriate barriers and entry controls;
  • Entry to secure areas is restricted to those whose job requires it;
  • Our offices are protected from power supply failures, as well as intruder alarms and fire suppression systems;
  • Door lock codes to secure areas are changed periodically, and the same measure is applied if suspicions arise that a code has been compromised;
  • All office doors, keys, access codes are, at all times, kept secure and never given or communicated to third parties;
  • Training is required for all levels of staff to make sure that everybody at Match Systems understands their data protection responsibilities;
  • Contract administration ensures that contracts with service providers offer accurate protection of personal data;
  • Confidentiality clauses are included in contracts to ensure that our employees and subcontractors keep your personal information confidential;
  • Virus scans and firewalls are installed to review and identify technological threats that could affect our network;
  • Access to the network is preformed via secure log-on procedure, with numerous passcodes, designed to minimize the opportunity for unauthorized access;
  • All email exchanges are encrypted to maintain confidentiality;
  • All data held electronically is securely and regularly backed up;
  • Automated security tests ensure that all software is subject to verification of security measures.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

We may transfer your personal data to third countries because of contractual relationships between Match Systems and our service providers. We make sure that the transfer is made in context of the contractual relationship and only if absolutely necessary. We also make sure to establish contractual relationships only with service providers from those countries that offer the highest degree of protection of personal data.

Deletion of Your Personal Data

The period for which customer data will be retained is dependent upon any statutory retention periods Match Systems is required to adhere to under applicable law. After the expiration of that period personal data shall be securely deleted, as long as it is no longer required for the fulfillment of any contract or in relation to other legal proceedings.
